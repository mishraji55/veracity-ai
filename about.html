About Veracity AI

A hackathon project dedicated to combating misinformation

The Problem: Misinformation in India

Rapid Spread

Misinformation spreads 6X faster than accurate information on social media platforms, reaching millions before fact-checkers can respond.

Mass Impact

India's 750+ million internet users are exposed to false information daily, affecting public health decisions, elections, and social harmony.

Real Consequences

False medical information endangers lives, while political misinformation destabilizes communities and undermines trust in institutions.

Current Challenges:

  • Information Overload: Millions of pieces of content shared daily make manual verification impossible
  • Language Barrier: Most misinformation in India is in Indian languages, which traditional fact-checkers struggle to cover
  • Media Literacy Gap: Many citizens lack tools and knowledge to verify information independently
  • Cost of Fact-Checking: Professional fact-checking is expensive and only covers a fraction of false claims

Our Mission

"To empower every citizen with AI-powered tools to verify information and make informed decisions in India."

Our Goals

  • Provide instant credibility analysis for news and content
  • Identify and flag specific misinformation tactics
  • Educate users about misinformation and media literacy
  • Make fact-checking accessible to everyone for free

Future Vision

  • Multi-language support for all Indian languages
  • Mobile app for easy access on smartphones
  • Integration with social media platforms
  • Real-time misinformation tracking and reporting

Why We Built Veracity AI

During this hackathon, we realized that the fight against misinformation requires accessible, intelligent tools that empower ordinary citizens. While AI and fact-checking technology exist, they're often:

Behind paywalls

Professional fact-checking services are expensive and inaccessible to many

Limited to English

Most tools don't support Indian languages where misinformation thrives

Not user-friendly

Complex interfaces make them difficult for average users to navigate

Geographically limited

Most fact-checking coverage focuses on Western countries

Veracity AI was born from the belief that misinformation is a universal problem that needs a universal solution. We've created a demo that shows how AI can be democratized to help everyone verify information, understand misinformation tactics, and become more informed citizens.
