Frequently Asked Questions

Is Veracity AI free to use?

Yes! Veracity AI is completely free to use. Our mission is to democratize fact-checking and combat misinformation, so we don't charge users for access.

How accurate is the verification?

Our AI analyzes multiple factors including source credibility, language patterns, and factual accuracy. While no system is 100% accurate, we strive for high precision. For critical decisions, we recommend verifying important claims through multiple sources.

Can I share results from Veracity AI?

Absolutely! We encourage users to share Veracity AI results to help fight misinformation. Results can be shared on social media, via messaging, or through email. Help spread verified information!

Do you store my data?

We take privacy seriously. Your submitted content is analyzed but not permanently stored. We only use it for verification and never share it with third parties. For more details, please check our privacy policy.

How can I contribute to Veracity AI?

We're always looking for passionate individuals! Whether it's development, design, content creation, or spreading awareness, there are many ways to contribute. Contact us at hello@veracity-ai.com to learn more about partnership opportunities.
