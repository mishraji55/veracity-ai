How It Works

Understanding Veracity AI's three-step verification process

1

Input News

Simply paste a news headline, article excerpt, or social media post into Veracity AI. Our system accepts any text content - from tweets to full articles.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced machine learning models evaluate the content across multiple dimensions: source credibility, language patterns, factual accuracy, and emotional manipulation tactics.

3

Credibility Report

Receive a detailed report showing your credibility score, risk level, and actionable insights about potential misinformation tactics used in the content.

Detailed Analysis Process

Source Analysis

We analyze the credibility of sources mentioned in your content:

  • Publishing history and reputation
  • Known fact-check scores
  • Editorial standards
  • Verification against known databases

Language & Tone

We examine linguistic patterns to detect misinformation tactics:

  • Emotional manipulation indicators
  • Sensational language detection
  • Bias identification
  • All-caps and punctuation analysis

Factual Accuracy

We cross-reference claims against reliable databases:

  • Claim verification against fact-check sources
  • Statistical data validation
  • Quote accuracy checks
  • Context analysis

Pattern Recognition

Our AI learns common misinformation patterns:

  • Deepfake detection patterns
  • Manipulated media identification
  • Coordinated inauthentic behavior
  • Bot-generated content detection
